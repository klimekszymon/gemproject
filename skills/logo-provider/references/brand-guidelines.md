# Brand Guidelines

To ensure consistency, please follow these guidelines when using the official logo symbol:

## 1. Clear Space
Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the thickness of one of the bars in the symbol. No other elements should encroach on this space.

## 2. Minimum Size
The logo symbol should not be reproduced smaller than 32px in digital environments or 1/2 inch in print.

## 3. Prohibited Alterations
- Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo symbol.
- Do not change the logo's color from the official palette.
- Do not add drop shadows, glows, gradients, or other effects.
- Do not rotate or rearrange the bars.

## 4. Color Palette
- **Slate**: #333333 (primary for symbol)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (background or alternate use)

## 5. Usage
- Use the logo symbol on neutral or white backgrounds for best visibility.
- Do not combine the symbol with other graphic elements unless approved by the design team.

For detailed questions, contact the design team.
